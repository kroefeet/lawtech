What are my qualifications?

I am a librarian with experience in legal research, project management, graphic design, computer programming and more. I have 10+ years of experience as a librarian and a lifetime of curiosity.

Why do I care?

I think there is an idea that librarians are supposed to be trustworthy human beings that exist to support people (all people). I feel like technology has made an effort to try to replace librarians in the area of information and research while also undermining the general population. I hope to bring a bit of awareness to the costs of technology in every day life and help others get a grip on what they are doing with technology and why they are doing it.

Why should you care?

You should care about this because technology is all around us. We all need to choose the technology we use and think about why we are using it.
